## 🚀 Overview
CapCut for Windows brings the popular editing experience to the desktop, where a bigger screen and more processing power make creative work faster and more comfortable. While many people first meet CapCut on mobile or online, the CapCut desktop app is designed for longer projects and precise editing: you get a spacious multi-track timeline, detailed trimming controls, and a full set of effects and transitions. It keeps the friendly, template-driven workflow that made CapCut popular — so beginners can start from a CapCut template and finish quickly — while giving advanced editors the room to layer audio, refine timing, and export polished, high-resolution video. It's a well-rounded video editor for creators who want to do more on a PC.

## ✨ Features
- **Full desktop timeline** — arrange and trim clips across multiple tracks with the precision a larger screen allows.
- **Template library** — begin with a CapCut template to build trend-ready videos, then customize the clips, music, and text.
- **Rich effects toolkit** — enhance footage with filters, transitions, and visual effects for a professional finish.
- **Audio and text tools** — layer soundtracks, sync audio, and add animated captions and titles to your videos.
- **Flexible export** — output your project at high resolution, ready for any platform you publish to.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is CapCut free?**
Yes, CapCut is free to download and use for editing on your desktop. A CapCut Pro tier adds extra features and content, but the free version covers a complete editing workflow.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
The CapCut desktop app runs on 64-bit Windows, mainly Windows 10 and Windows 11. Using a supported Windows version is recommended.

**Is the desktop version different from CapCut online?**
The desktop app is built for more involved editing on a PC, with a full timeline and local processing, while CapCut online offers quick edits in a browser. Both share the familiar CapCut style.

**Is my data secure?**
CapCut lets you edit locally on your Windows PC. Sign in with a trusted account, keep the app updated, and store your exported videos like any other files on your computer.
